NARRATIVE
================================================================================

On February 10, 2024, at approximately 14:30 hours, this office received a 
report of theft at a commercial establishment located at Barangay San Jose, 
Quezon City.

The complainant, Mr. Roberto Santos, reported that his store was broken into 
during the night of February 9-10, 2024. Initial investigation revealed:

1. Forced entry through the rear door
2. Missing items include:
   - Cash amounting to PHP 25,000.00
   - Electronic devices valued at PHP 45,000.00
   - Various merchandise worth PHP 30,000.00
   Total estimated loss: PHP 100,000.00

3. Evidence collected:
   - Fingerprints from the point of entry
   - CCTV footage from nearby establishments
   - Witness statements from three individuals
